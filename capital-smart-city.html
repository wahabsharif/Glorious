Introducing Capital Smart City

Capital Smart City is a sophisticated and contemporary residential project, distinguished as Pakistan's first smart city and ranked 4th in Asia. With its array of smart features, it is rapidly gaining prominence as one of Pakistan's most renowned housing societies. The concept of Capital Smart City revolves around the utilization of real-time data through its collection, archiving, and analysis. Developed by Habib Rafiq and FDHL, this project is conveniently situated near the New Islamabad International Airport, adjacent to the Lahore-Islamabad Motorway (M-2).

The Smart City Project in Islamabad goes beyond serving the local population of Pakistan, as it includes a dedicated zone specifically designed for Overseas Pakistanis and individuals with multiple nationalities.

Capital Smart City is the pioneering housing scheme to introduce an 18-lane or 400 ft Main Boulevard, setting a new standard of advanced, innovative, and functional living for the people of Pakistan. This innovative housing culture has garnered significant attention due to its impressive array of smart features and amenities provided to the residents.

Ownership and Developers of Capital Smart City

Capital Smart City Islamabad is the result of a collaborative effort between Habib Rafiq (Pvt) Ltd. and Future Development Holdings (Pvt) Limited. Through their partnership, Pakistan now boasts smart cities that rival the amenities found in major global cities such as New York, Copenhagen, Berlin, and Singapore. The joint developers are widely recognized for their expertise and capabilities, having successfully delivered multiple projects in the past, including Bahria Town, DHA in Islamabad, and various other notable ventures.

Capital Smart City Location

The location of Capital Smart City is highly advantageous, situated at the intersection of Chakri Road and M-2 Motorway. It offers convenient access via its dedicated interchange on the M-2 Motorway, providing a direct link to the society. Additionally, access to the society can also be gained from Thalian Interchange.

Capital Smart City NOC

The Smart City Project in Islamabad is a legally approved housing scheme, as it obtained its NOC from the Rawalpindi Development Authority (RDA) in September 2019. Unlike many neighboring housing schemes, which have not received approval from the relevant authorities, Capital Smart City's ability to obtain its NOC within a remarkably short period of time is a testament to the project's authenticity. Typically, the process of acquiring an NOC from the RDA takes several months.

Furthermore, Capital Smart City has also obtained approval for the Sui Gas Network. The grant of Sui gas supply was finalized in 2023.

Payment Plan

Capital Smart City, located in Islamabad, has emerged as a prominent real estate development and is highly sought-after within the region. In 2023, the community has introduced an updated payment plan, along with a revised Master Plan and Map, catering to the evolving needs of potential investors.

The payment plan offers flexibility and is tailored to different plot sizes, enabling interested individuals to invest in the community's projects. It allows for monthly installments over a period of three years, devoid of any interest charges. Additionally, a one-time registration fee is included in the payment plan.

The revised payment plan for 2023 specifically focuses on the residential blocks, namely Overseas East, Overseas Central, and Overseas Prime 1. These blocks are designed to cater to the requirements of overseas Pakistanis interested in investing in the Overseas District. Similar to the general payment plan, these blocks also involve a one-time registration fee.

Furthermore, Smart City Islamabad provides numerous plots for sale in both the commercial and residential sectors, offering prospective buyers a wide range of options to choose from.
