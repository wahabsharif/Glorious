About Us

BSM Developers has gained widespread recognition for their opulent and contemporary housing projects, offering a tranquil and contented living environment to residents. Notably, two of their major projects, "Gwadar Golf City" in Gwadar and "New Metro City" in Kharian Sarai Alamgir, have captivated the public's attention.

New Metro City in Kharian Sarai Alamgir has been particularly remarkable, providing an array of amenities such as mosques, schools, parks, jogging tracks, top-notch security, and shopping malls. The strategic location, impressive development, and reasonable pricing have added to its allure and appeal. Building on the success of these two projects, BSM Developers is excited to announce their forthcoming project in Gujjar Khan.

Introducing the new and improved New Metro City Gujjar Khan, BSM Developers aims to create an even more vibrant and refined living experience at an affordable price. With a commitment to providing all essential facilities, this project aims to enhance the quality of life and ensure maximum comfort for families residing in the area.

This mega-development aims to present a blend of luxury and affordability, providing a splendid housing scheme accessible to a wide range of homebuyers. Its prime location next to the GT Road offers an excellent investment opportunity near Gujar Khan. Moreover, the project's proximity to Islamabad, with just a few hours' drive, further enhances its appeal and convenience. New Metro City Gujar Khan promises to be a significant step in bridging the gap between opulence and budget-friendly living options.

New Metro City Gujar Khan Developers & Owners

BSM Developers PVT LTD, led by Bilal Bashir Malik, the grandson of Malik Riaz (owner of Bahria Town), is the visionary force behind the creation of New Metro City Gujar Khan. Following in the footsteps of their esteemed predecessors, the company has already achieved success with their Golf City Gwadar project and is now embarking on the journey of Metro City.

Their vision for Metro City revolves around an eco-friendly housing project that harmoniously blends modern amenities with affordability. With a commitment to providing all the essential facilities, this housing scheme is designed to offer a comfortable and contemporary living experience at budget-friendly rates. BSM Developers is dedicated to carrying forward the legacy of their forefathers with the same zeal and enthusiasm, aiming to create a thriving community in New Metro City Gujar Khan.

NOC

The Rawalpindi Development Authority (RDA) has officially granted its approval for the NOC (No Objection Certificate) of New Metro City Gujjar Khan. With this significant milestone achieved, potential investors can now rest assured and have peace of mind when considering investing in this exceptional housing community.

Location

The location of any housing society plays a pivotal role, and the New Metro City Gujar Khan has been meticulously designed at one of the most sought-after and prime positions within the city. Positioned near Islamabad, right on the main GT Road, this project enjoys a highly desirable location. Its main entrance directly on GT Road, along with multiple secondary entrances from other roads, ensures convenient access.

The strategic placement of New Metro City Gujar Khan provides effortless connectivity to key highways in the twin cities region, making it easy to navigate and explore. Residents and investors alike benefit from the society's fantastic area, offering a superb living experience and promising investment opportunities. The seamless accessibility to essential amenities and commercial establishments adds to its appeal, making it an attractive choice for anyone seeking a quality lifestyle in proximity to Islamabad.

With its advantageous location and reasonable pricing, this housing society stands out as an exceptional investment opportunity for those looking to embrace a comfortable and well-connected lifestyle near the capital city.

New Metro City Gujar Khan Master Plan

A team of highly experienced professionals, including town planners, engineers, developers, management, and other experts, meticulously planned, designed, and developed the 4,000 Kanal master plan of New Metro City Gujar Khan. Their collective expertise and dedication went into crafting this well-thought-out and comprehensive layout for the housing project.
