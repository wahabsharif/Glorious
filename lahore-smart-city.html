Lahore Smart City

Lahore Smart City (LSC) is an approved housing society by the Lahore Development Authority (LDA), situated at Lahore Bypass. It is a collaborative project between FDH (Future Development Holdings) and HRL (Habib Rafiq Limited). LSC has introduced a new standard of living in the real estate industry, offering modern facilities and amenities.

The housing society stands out with its impressive architecture, luxurious lifestyle, state-of-the-art construction, forward-thinking planning, smart engineering solutions, and efficient designs, making it an ideal investment opportunity in Lahore.

LSC boasts a 20-mile recreational trail along the eco-spine and rivers, creating a seamless network for residents to enjoy. It features a 600 Kanal central park that integrates a grand mosque and a financial square, serving as a tourist destination. The housing society also ensures a generous allocation of 20m2 open space per person, incorporating a range of sports, cultural, community, and garden facilities.

HRL and FDHL have collaborated to develop Lahore Smart City as a prime investment opportunity, offering a comprehensive and modern living experience in Lahore.

Developers & Owners

Lahore Smart City, a prestigious project, was jointly developed by renowned companies including Future Development Holdings PVT Ltd. (FDHL), Surbana Jurong (SJ), and Habib Rafique Pvt. Limited (HRL). These developers have an impressive track record of constructing world-class property projects both in Pakistan and abroad.

Their reputation in the real estate industry is well-established due to their unwavering dedication and commitment to delivering high-quality infrastructure in residential societies like DHA (Defence Housing Authority) and Bahria Town. Moreover, they have successfully established entire housing societies independently, such as Royal Orchard Sahiwal, Royal Orchard Sargodha, and Royal Orchard Multan.

Lahore Smart City NOC

Lahore Smart City has obtained the necessary approval from the Lahore Development Authority (LDA) in the form of a No Objection Certificate (NOC). The NOC, with the reference number LDA/DMP-IV/700, was issued on April 8, 2021. Recently, the LDA has updated the NOC status of Lahore Smart City on its official website. This approval signifies that the housing society has a greater likelihood of successful development and fulfillment of its objectives.

Lahore Smart City Master Plan

The master plan of Lahore Smart City was meticulously crafted, designed, and developed by a team of highly skilled and qualified experts. Their dedicated efforts were aimed at creating a comprehensive lifestyle for the residents. Lahore Smart City is organized into multiple districts and blocks, each offering unique features and amenities.

Initially, the management made the decision to introduce two residential areas within the society, equipped with modern facilities. Following this decision, the development work commenced simultaneously in both of the launched blocks, ensuring progress and advancement in the construction and infrastructure of the residential areas.

Executive Block

Lahore Smart City has recently launched the Executive Block, providing exceptional living and investment opportunities. This thoughtfully designed block offers a wide range of facilities to enhance the quality of life for its residents. Within the Executive Block, one can find schools, colleges, clinics, restaurants, parks, shopping areas, and various other amenities.

In terms of residential plots, the Executive Block offers options ranging from 5, 7, 10, 12 Marla to 1 Kanal. Additionally, there are 4 and 8 Marla commercial plots available. The booking process for residential plots in the Executive Block is currently open, with the option to make easy installments.

Development work has already commenced in this block, ensuring timely progress and the realization of the envisioned infrastructure.
