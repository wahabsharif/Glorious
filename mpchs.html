Multi-Professional Co-operative Housing Scheme

The Multi-Professional Co-operative Housing Scheme (MPCH) serves as a platform for promoting real estate investment and developing high-standard housing schemes. It functions as a joint platform for buyers and sellers, fostering transparent deals that eventually lead to dividends for all parties involved.

Driven by the growing membership, MPCH ventured into the development of Multi Gardens Sector B-17, which stands as the largest scheme within the Society, boasting a masterfully planned layout.

Located as a gateway to Islamabad, Sector B-17 offers convenient access from upcountry regions, including Afghanistan, Central Asia, and China, through Margalla Avenue. The sector features modern amenities that contribute to a comfortable lifestyle.

Multi Gardens B-17 provides all essential facilities and amenities, such as utilities, schools, mosques, parks, and shopping areas. It is a well-developed and populated society.

Efficient access to the society is ensured through the Motorway interchange and the under-construction Margalla Road. Furthermore, its close proximity to the Islamabad International Airport positions this housing scheme as an ideal residential address in Islamabad.

Multi-Professional Cooperative Housing Society's Multi-Gardens in B-17, Islamabad, gained significant popularity in 2018 and continues to maintain its position as one of the country's most sought-after housing societies. Despite the current slowdown in the real estate market, the society remains resilient, witnessing active buying and selling activities and sustained investor interest.

Development Update and Investment Perspective

Traditionally, MPCHS has conducted balloting after the completion of two installments, and the same is expected for Block G. The second installment is set to be due in January 2019, indicating that balloting and the release of the map are likely to take place around late January or February. Substantial development is expected to follow thereafter.

Block D remains in a similar state as before, with no significant changes. However, Block F has experienced notable progress, with over 90% of the development completed, including ongoing road carpeting.

Regarding investment opportunities, almost all blocks in Multi-Gardens B-17 hold potential. However, the newer blocks may be more suitable for consideration. Block G, in particular, offers relatively lower prices and a significant number of remaining installments, making it a more affordable option.

For those with a higher budget, other options to explore include Block F, where plots still have remaining installments and possession is available in a large portion of the block. Additionally, Blocks D and E present opportunities, with anticipated price increases in the future.
