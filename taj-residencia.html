About Us

Taj Residencia in Islamabad is the upcoming luxury living accommodation that promises an exceptional experience. Backed by the renowned Sardar Group of Companies, known for delivering successful projects, Taj Residencia is expected to surpass expectations and set new standards in luxury living.

With their proven success in projects like Centaurus Mall, which has become both a luxurious residential project and a commercial hub in Islamabad, the Sardar Group is determined to replicate this success with Taj Residencia. This remarkable project assures impressive results and aims to offer a glorious living experience comparable to Centaurus Mall, but at even more competitive rates.

The construction of Taj Residencia in Islamabad is being carried out by the esteemed Sardar Group of Companies, a pioneer in the premium residential development market of Pakistan. With a track record of originating, planning, and building exceptional projects, the Group made history in 2013 by introducing Pakistan to a world-class marvel, "The Centaurus Mall" in Islamabad. This cutting-edge super-enterprise surpassed all previous standards and delivered an unparalleled performance, setting new benchmarks in the country's real estate development.

The Sardar Group of Companies has a proven track record of success and a clear vision for creating premium housing developments and commercial districts in Islamabad, as exemplified by their iconic project, Centaurus Mall. Taj Residency is yet another remarkable venture that guarantees impressive results. Taj aims to offer an abundant and luxurious experience similar to Centaurus Mall, but at a fraction of the cost, making it an attractive investment opportunity.

Taj Residencia Developers

Mr. Sardar Ilyas Khan, the owner of Taj Residencia, is a highly influential figure in the real estate industry. As the president of the Islamabad Chamber of Commerce and Industry (ICCI), he holds a prominent position in the business community.

Mr. Sardar is a formidable business tycoon who has revolutionized real estate practices in Pakistan. Over the past decades, the Sardar Group of Companies has consistently surpassed the expectations of clients and investors.

The company's hallmark of quality and dedication can be seen in the iconic Centaurus Mall, which stands as a symbol of their success in the Federal Capital. Currently, the company is embarking on a splendid venture, Taj Residencia, near Islamabad, which is poised to become the most illustrious housing society in Pakistan.

Taj Residencia Master Plan

The developers of Taj Residencia have thoughtfully designed the property to cater to residents' comfort and daily needs without compromising on their lifestyles. The layout incorporates well-planned highways, making commuting convenient. The property features parks and business zones, ensuring a balanced and self-sufficient living environment, almost like a city within a city.

Phase 1 of Taj Residencia has been unveiled, with ample space reserved for Phase 2 and potential future expansions. The available properties include residential plots, condos, and villas. While the growth rate may be moderate compared to other housing units in the suburbs, Taj Residencia covers a substantial area of 1907 Kanals of protected land. The initial phase is divided into several sections, primarily offering residential and commercial plots. Additionally, Taj Residencia offers Taj Villas in Contemporary, Classical, and Eclectic styles, with varying sizes ranging from 5 to 20 Marla regions.
