Zarkon - The Jewel

Zarkon Heights, located in Islamabad's G-15 sector, is a luxurious apartment-based housing scheme that presents an excellent opportunity for both residential living and investment. Drawing inspiration from the awe-inspiring Egyptian architecture, this housing complex places a special emphasis on incorporating geometric designs throughout. Standing tall in Islamabad's skyline, Zarkon Heights offers its residents a range of top-notch amenities that meet international standards

Located in close proximity to the M-2 highway and Metro Station, Zarkon Heights offers convenient access to the New International Islamabad Airport, GT Road, and Zero Point, all just a few minutes away. This advantageous location in Islamabad, combined with state-of-the-art amenities and a secure environment, positions Zarkon Heights as the top choice for a sought-after residential community in the capital city of Pakistan.

About Developers

Drawing upon their extensive experience of more than 20 years, Kohistan Builders & Developers (KBD) has successfully created Zarkon Heights as a symbol of unparalleled excellence. KBD serves as the leading entity within the esteemed Kohistan Group of Companies, which is actively involved in a diverse range of industries including logistics, manufacturing, petroleum, hospitality, and real estate.

Established in 1995, KBD has leveraged the expertise of its exceptional team consisting of skilled managers, engineers, and field staff to successfully deliver notable residential and commercial projects throughout Pakistan. As a significant milestone, KBD has recently accomplished the construction of its inaugural 4-Star Hotel in its city of origin.

Location

Situated in sector G-15, Zarkon Heights enjoys a prime location near the junction of Kashmir Highway and GT Road in Islamabad. Additionally, it benefits from its close proximity to Motorway M2 and the new Islamabad International Airport. Conveniently accessible from Zero Point and Blue Area, Zarkon Heights holds a strategic position in Islamabad, being in close proximity to major centers and offering easy access to all the key areas of the city.

GLORIOUS EXPERT OPINION

Zarkon Heights is an exceptional project situated on the outskirts of G-15. It holds the esteemed approval of the Capital Development Authority (CDA) and is located within a CDA sector of G-15. If you are seeking an apartment with a valid No Objection Certificate (NOC), positioned in a CDA sector, and equipped with all necessary features and luxuries, Zarkon Heights is the ultimate choice.

Tower A and B have been fully completed and are available for immediate occupancy. However, please note that these towers are only available for resale and full cash payments, without installment options.

For those interested in booking an apartment and prefer installment plans, Tower E is the most suitable option.

Overall, Zarkon Heights is a remarkable project in a superb location, with easy access from the main road. Once the Margalla Road is completed, it will further enhance connectivity to major locations in Islamabad and Rawalpindi. Developed by the reputable Kohistan Builders, known for their investment in projects like Eighteen and F-11 Karakoram Heights, as well as their upcoming mega project Kohistan Tower in Saddar, this project stands out with its wide range of offerings. I would personally rate this project as a solid 9.5/10, as it leaves little to be desired.

DEVELOPMENT STATUS

Currently, Zarkon Heights comprises a total of five buildings. Tower A and B have been fully constructed and sold out, while Tower C, which has a grey structure, is also fully completed and sold out. Tower D and F are currently under construction, although they have already been sold out. The only ongoing bookings are available for Tower E, which is scheduled to be completed within the next two years.
