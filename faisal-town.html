Faisal Town

Faisal Town marks the pioneering project driven by the unwavering confidence and trust placed in the vision, expertise, and delivery capabilities of our CEO, Ch. Abdul Majeed. With approval from RDA and adherence to all legal formalities, Faisal Town is ideally located on Main Fateh Jang Road, N-80, near the Tarnol Interchange Motorway M-1 in Rawalpindi.

For numerous individuals, residing in Faisal Town Phase 2 is like a cherished dream turned into reality. This city has rapidly gained popularity as one of the most coveted residential areas in the nation, thanks to its exceptional infrastructure and opulent amenities. The architectural aspects of Faisal Town Phase 2 undergo continuous updates, incorporating community-oriented and environmentally friendly features, as well as introducing innovative concepts. The housing development places great emphasis on stunning designs and architecture, making it a standout among others. With its remarkable potential, the entire project is poised to attain a prominent position in Pakistan's real estate market.

Faisal Town boasts two impressive main entrance gates, each spanning a width of 110 feet. These gates are meticulously designed, showcasing a captivating fusion of the revered Gandhara traditions and the timeless Mughal architectural style. At Faisal Town, we prioritize our clients' architectural preferences and requirements, ensuring that their needs are at the forefront of our designs.

Zedem International has commenced the initial development of Faisal Town Phase 2, aiming to create a luxurious living experience. Following the remarkable success of our esteemed project, Faisal Town Islamabad, and the enthusiastic response received from the public, we have introduced Faisal Town Phase 2 to cater to the growing demand from homebuyers and investors. Situated at the Thalian Interchange, this vibrant community is set to redefine modern living.

Owner & Developer of Faisal Town

Faisal Town Islamabad is under the ownership of Chaudhry Abdul Majeed, renowned for his successful delivery of various residential projects in Islamabad such as E-11, F-17, B-17, Faisal Hills, Faisal Residencia, and Faisal Margalla City.

The esteemed developer behind Faisal Town is Zedem International Private Limited, known for their outstanding track record in real estate development. Zedem International Pvt Ltd has also accomplished other notable projects including Faisal Town, Faisal Residencia, Faisal Tower, and SEA Square. Over the years, the company has experienced rapid growth and has earned a respected position among real estate developers.

Faisal Town - Block C

Location

Situated prominently on the primary Fatehjang-Kohat Road, Faisal Town Phase 2 finds its place adjacent to the Fateh Jang Interchange on Motorway M-1. The location holds significant advantages as it is conveniently located merely a 10-minute drive away from the New Islamabad International Airport and a short 5-minute drive away from the N-5 GT Road. Additionally, the vicinity benefits from the presence of Kashmir Highway and Margalla Avenue, both of which offer swift and direct access routes to the city.

NOC

Faisal Town Islamabad has obtained the necessary No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the Rawalpindi Development Authority (RDA), ensuring its compliance with regulatory requirements. Approved housing projects like Faisal Town have a greater likelihood of achieving successful sales and attracting investors. Real estate ventures that have obtained all the required NOCs from relevant authorities are trusted by investors, as it provides assurance that their investments are secure and have the potential for profitability.
